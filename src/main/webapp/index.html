Our flagship programs

OneLive is an interactive webinar series done every weekend with the experts in their fields. "OneLive" is a trademark program of SEF with the vsion to expand the knowledge of Sri Lankan students and it will allow you to interact with the speaker via asking questions live.

ScholarX is an exclusive 3-month program aimed at providing mentoring support to a selected pool of High Potential undergraduate students based in Sri Lanka ideally by a Sri Lankan expat currently engaged with one of the world’s top universities or Fortune 500 companies. It’s our free premium mentoring platform by Sri Lankans for Sri Lankans working towards creating a culture of knowledge and expertise sharing without the limitation of geographical borders.

The International Research Council (IRC) is a community created by the Sustainable Education Foundation (SEF) to showcase the work of Sri Lankan researchers. One of the main goals of the IRC is to provide a digital platform to connect our community of researchers who are actively pursuing research projects in various fields all over the world.
