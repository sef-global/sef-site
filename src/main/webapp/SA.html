STUDENT AMBASSADOR

Student Ambassadors (SA) of the Sustainable Education Foundation (SEF) will act as a liaison for SEF while also acting as an Evangelist for the SEF brand among your peers. This position is for student leaders who are passionate about developing their employability skills and sharing knowledge.


APPLY

Frequently Asked Questions

SA’s will be trained and mentored throughout the program via our skills development program formulated with industry experts to give you the in-demand skills employers are seeking for like time management, adaptability and collaboration. The program will arm you to be career ready when you graduate from university. You will also be able to develop your leadership, project management and marketing skills when you deliver SEF programs at campus level.
  • Conduct on campus sessions to spread the word on SEF in your respective Universities
  • Build a strong, sustainable SEF community and collaboration within other clubs/societies in your respective university
  • Share the work that SEF is doing among your peers via social media platforms
  • Blog/Vlog about your experience with SEF
  • Maintain the SEF brand name internally and externally from the University
  • Volunteer in a SEF core team/work stream
  • A high degree of multi-tasking and time management capability
  • Good communication skills in English and Sinhala or Tamil
  • Exemplary leadership qualities
